EXAMPLE


    ls -l /home/james
    total 3
    drwxr-xr-x  2 james users 4096 Mar 23 13:34 bin
    drwxr-xr-x 18 james users 4096 Feb 17 09:12 Documents
    drwxr-xr-x  2 james users 4096 May 05 17:25 public_html

Line 1:
    Command line arguments, and the first ARG is usually
    the [option]
    Usually used to modify the behaviour of the Command.
    Listed before ARGs and start with a dash[-]
Line 2-5:
    Outputs from running the command.
    Most produce output and it'll be listed under the
    issued command.
Line 6:
    Gives the command prompt again. After command is ran
    and terminal is then ready to recieve another command.
